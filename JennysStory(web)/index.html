Jenny's Story

About this special book!



The story of Jenny is based off what some of us eat on a daily basis. Even though it's good to have something sweet, sugary, salty, oily or meaty, eating them on a daily basis can cause some serious problems later in life! Jenny does just that with her choices on what to eat.
